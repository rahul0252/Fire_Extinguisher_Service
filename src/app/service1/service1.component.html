Be Prepared, Be Safe : Fire Emergencies on Trains

The fire incidences in trains are among the most serious disasters to human lives and the property of Indian Railways. A train fire is different from a fire in other places in the manner in which it breaks out, grows and spreads, and in the method of fighting it. Fire in a coach on running train is very dangerous because it spreads very quickly to other coaches due to fanning effect.
